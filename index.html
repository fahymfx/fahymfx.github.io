Creative Standards

My Work Ethics

Clear creative policies that guide every project I take on

Halal Audio Only

No musical instruments. I use high-quality acapella, nature sounds, or 'Halal Beats' (vocal percussion) for rhythm.

Strictly Enforced

Respectful Visuals

No drawings or AI-generated images of animate beings with full facial features. Features are blurred or silhouetted.

Strictly Enforced

Modesty & Decency

I do not edit footage containing nudity, alcohol, gambling, or any haram elements. Clean content only.

Strictly Enforced

Sharia-Compliant Editing

All content is curated to align with Islamic principles, ensuring your message is ethical and impactful.

Strictly Enforced
